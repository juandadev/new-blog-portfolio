## What I'm doing now

Last updated: April 2026

### Where I am

Living, working, and creating in Guadalajara, Mexico.

### The Main Focus: Becoming a Design Engineer

While my day job as a platform frontend developer remains stable, I am currently locking in on my transition into a
Design Engineer. Because AI is now handling the heavy lifting of my daily engineering workflows, I finally have the time
and mental space to merge my two biggest passions: development and design. I've always wanted to be a designer, and I'm
incredibly excited to be building the knowledge and portfolio needed to land my dream role.

### Life, Art & Finding Balance

I am actively breaking the habit of sitting at a desk for 12 hours a day. To stimulate my brain, build new neural
pathways, and fuel my design work, I'm focusing heavily on a balanced, offline life:

- Reclaiming my roots: I'm going back to the artistic hobbies I loved in high school—playing guitar, drawing, and
  painting—to explore creativity outside of a screen.
- Health: Staying disciplined with my diet and physical fitness, getting back into Olympic weightlifting with my home
  setup.
- Analog focus: Still dialing in my daily espresso extraction on the Bambino, and unwinding with pixel-art
  cross-stitching.
